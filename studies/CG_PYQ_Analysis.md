## Key Observations

1. **Modules 3 and 4 dominate** all available PYQs — the papers are B-series showing only these modules.
2. **Cohen-Sutherland, Depth Buffer, and Convolution** are asked in ALL 4 papers — guaranteed exam topics.
3. **Part B numericals are increasing** — 2025 had numerical convolution and Cohen-Sutherland. Be ready to apply, not just explain.
4. **Module 1 & 2 PYQs are absent** from these papers (Bresenham's, DDA, 2D/3D transformations) — these are likely tested in other series. Do not neglect them.
5. **Module 5 (Image Enhancement & Segmentation)** — completely absent from these papers. Likely tested in A-series. High risk area.
